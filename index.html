Who We Are

We are a community playscheme supporting primary-aged children and families.

Our purpose is to combat holiday hunger while providing a safe, welcoming environment where children can enjoy play, build friendships, and develop social skills.

What We Do

🍽️ Holiday Hunger Support

Providing meals and care during school holidays.

🎨 Play & Learning

Fun, creative activities that support development.

🤝 Social Skills

Helping children build confidence and friendships.
